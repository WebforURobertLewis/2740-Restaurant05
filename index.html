Our History of Pies

Poland - Blueberry Pie

Our increadible pies each have an amazing history! For instance, our coveted blueberry pie recipe came from Poland! A wonderful and well loved old lady passed away and left her secret recipe to her family. They then sent the recipe to us! It has had over 100 years of adjustment until the perfect combination of bluebrry and cream was created!
This was the first pie we ever introduced to our customers.

China - Strawberry Pie

What happens when you combine more than 100 years of strawberry growing experience with talented chefs? You get our mouth-watering Strawberry Pie! Recently, China has been known for it's strawberry growing industry, which means that with their vast quantity of straweberries and chefs, they have been able to create some increadible pies! We have chosen the strawberry pie we think is the best! Let us know if you think the same!
